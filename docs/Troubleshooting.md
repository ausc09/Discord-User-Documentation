# Troubleshooting Guide

| Problem | Possible Cause | Solution |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| You did not receive the verification email after creating your account. | The email may have gone to your spam or junk folder, or you entered the wrong email address. | Check your spam or junk folder. If the email is not there, return to Discord and click [Resend Email]. Make sure your email address is entered correctly. |
| You cannot find your classmate when sending a friend request. | The username was entered incorrectly, or the user has privacy settings that restrict friend requests. | Double-check the exact username with your classmate, including capitalization. Ask them to send the friend request to you instead. |
| You cannot hear others during a voice meeting. | Your output device may be incorrectly configured in Discord, or your speaker/headphone volume is too low. | Go to [User Settings] → [Voice & Video] and check that the correct output device is selected. Also check your system volume. |
| Others cannot hear you during a voice meeting. | Your microphone may be muted in Discord, or the wrong input device is selected. | Check that the microphone icon in the voice channel is not crossed out. Go to [User Settings] → [Voice & Video] and confirm the correct input device is selected. |
| The invite link is not working for your teammates. | The link may have expired or reached its maximum number of uses. | Return to [Server Settings] → [Invites] and generate a new invite link. Make sure to set an appropriate expiration time and usage limit. |
| You cannot create a voice channel or change server settings. | You may not have the required permissions in the server. | Contact the server owner to grant you the appropriate role or permissions. |
| Your screen share is lagging or freezing during the meeting. | Your internet connection may be unstable, or too many applications are running. | Close unnecessary applications to free up memory. Move closer to your Wi-Fi router or switch to a wired connection if possible. |
